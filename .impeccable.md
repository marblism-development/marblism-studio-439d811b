## Design Context

### Users
Solo founders — builders who move fast, make high-leverage decisions daily, and have no patience for noise. They want tools that respect their time and make them feel capable and in control.

### Brand Personality
Professional, clean, and functional. The voice is direct and confident — no fluff, no hype. Copy earns every word. The product feels like it was built by someone who uses it.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual tone & theme**: Dark mode first — deep near-black backgrounds with precise electric accents. Calm at rest, electric when it matters.
- **Color palette**: Background `#0A0B0F`, surface `#13151C`, border `#1E2130`, primary accent `#6366F1` (electric indigo), secondary `#0EA5E9` (sky blue), text `#F1F5F9`, muted `#64748B`
- **References**: No external references — derive from the palette and voice above.

### Site Purpose
A product built for solo founders: helping them move faster, build smarter, and ship with confidence. The landing page communicates immediate value, reduces friction, and drives a single clear action.

### Design Principles
1. **Clarity over decoration** — every element earns its place; white space is structural, not decorative.
2. **Electric restraint** — accent color appears sparingly so it always signals importance.
3. **Founder-speed copy** — headlines are declarative and outcome-focused, not feature-focused.
4. **Calm density** — information is organized, never crowded; a founder can scan and act in seconds.
